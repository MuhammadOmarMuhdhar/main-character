About maincharacter.day

maincharacter.day tracks the most talked-about people on Bluesky social media platform in real-time.

Our Mission

To provide transparent insights into social media discourse and help people understand what's capturing public attention.

The Team

Built by developers passionate about social media analytics and open data.

How It Works

Data Collection

We monitor the Bluesky firehose API to track all public posts and interactions in real-time.

Scoring System

Our algorithm analyzes mention frequency, reply-to-like ratios, and engagement patterns to identify trending individuals.

Update Frequency

Rankings are updated every hour based on the past 24 hours of activity.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the data?

Our data is sourced directly from Bluesky's public API and updated in real-time. However, scores reflect algorithmic analysis and may not capture all nuances of social discourse.

Why am I on this list?

You appear because you've been mentioned frequently or generated significant engagement on Bluesky in the past 24 hours.

Can I be removed from the list?

The list reflects public social media activity. If you'd like to be excluded, please contact us with your request.

Privacy & Data

Data We Collect

We only process publicly available data from Bluesky's API, including posts, usernames, and engagement metrics.

Data Processing

Data is processed to generate aggregated statistics and rankings. No private or personal information is stored beyond what's publicly available.

Your Rights

You have the right to request removal from our tracking. Contact us for opt-out requests.
